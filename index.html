FAQ
Is Epitaph non-custodial?+
Yes. Epitaph never holds your assets. Smart contracts execute autonomously. You maintain full control until trigger conditions are met.
What if I'm still alive after the trigger?+
You can cancel or update your will anytime before execution. Simply send a transaction from your wallet to reset the inactivity timer.
Can my will be changed after deployment?+
Yes — only by you, the wallet owner. Update beneficiaries, triggers, or revoke entirely. Once execution begins, it is immutable.
Is there a fee?+
Only gas fees to deploy. Epitaph takes 0.5% only upon successful execution. No subscriptions. No upfront costs.
Is the final message private?+
Yes. Encrypted on-chain, decryptable only by your beneficiaries after the trigger fires.
