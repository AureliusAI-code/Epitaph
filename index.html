FAQ
Is Epitaph non-custodial?+
Yes. Epitaph never holds your assets. Smart contracts execute autonomously. You maintain full control of your wallet until trigger conditions are met.
What happens if I'm still alive after the trigger?+
You can cancel or update your will at any time before it executes. Simply send a transaction from your wallet to reset the inactivity timer. You remain in full control.
Can my will be changed after deployment?+
Yes — but only by you, the wallet owner. You can update beneficiaries, trigger conditions, or revoke the will entirely. Once the trigger fires and execution begins, it becomes immutable.
Is there a fee?+
Deploying your will costs only gas fees. Epitaph takes a small protocol fee (0.5%) only upon successful execution. No subscriptions. No upfront costs.
Is the final message private?+
Yes. Your final message is encrypted on-chain and can only be decrypted by your designated beneficiaries after the trigger fires. Nobody else — including Epitaph — can read it.
